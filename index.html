5) Two Acquisition Models (Choose One)

Model A — Full Cash Payment

Highest margin • Simple

Best for investors seeking immediate ownership and simplified execution without bank repayment obligations.

  • Direct acquisition from authorized supplier (Long Tai)
  • Ownership can be personal or company-based
  • Operations executed under KivuRide (drivers + monitoring)

Model B — NCBA Financing

Scale faster • Bank program

This financing structure is program-aligned for drivers: the investor forms a company, onboards eligible drivers, and submits required documents via the company.

  • Loan principal: 100% financing
  • Deposit: 10% deposit submitted, refundable upon vehicle arrival + yellow card issuance (program mechanics)
  • Term: 5 years • Interest: 15% APR (compounded monthly)

NCBA Structure for Non-Driver Investors (Clear Requirement)

  • Investor establishes a company for fleet operation.
  • Company onboards licensed drivers who have taxi operating statements.
  • Driver + company documents are submitted under NCBA requirements.
  • Upon approval, vehicles are registered under the company and operated under KivuRide governance.
